## ðŸ“š Study Routine Template

### Daily (Monday-Friday)

**Morning Block (1 hour): Active Practice**
- 20 new questions from current resource
- Timed conditions (aim for 3-4 min per question)
- Mark immediately after
- Score: ___/20

**Afternoon Block (1 hour): Deep Review**
- Analyze EVERY mistake
- Understand WHY you got it wrong
- Find fastest solution method
- Create Anki cards (3-5 cards per session)

**Evening Block (30 min): Reinforcement**
- Anki reviews (all due cards)
- Re-attempt yesterday's mistakes
- Light reading of Logic & Proof notes

**Total:** 2.5 hours/day, 5 days/week = 12.5 hours/week

## ðŸ’¡ Top Tips for Beginners

### 1. Don't Peak Too Early
- Save official TMUA papers until Week 17+
- Use MAT, STEP, PAT for building skills
- Treat TMUA papers as "sacred" final practice

### 2. Mistakes Are Gold
- Every mistake = learning opportunity
- Don't just mark wrong answers and move on
- Understand the underlying concept

### 3. Speed Builds Gradually
- Week 1-4: Focus on accuracy (no time pressure)
- Week 5-8: Gradual time pressure (5 min/question)
- Week 9+: Strict timing (3.75 min/question)

### 4. Anki Is Your Best Friend
- Review EVERY DAY (even just 10 minutes)
- Better to create 5 great cards than 20 rushed ones
- Quality > Quantity

### 5. Rest Is Part of Training
- One full rest day per week
- Sleep 8 hours during prep
- Burnout helps nobody!
